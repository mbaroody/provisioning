Leaderboard Shutdown

After long consideration, I decided to shut down Anki Leaderboard on June 14, 2024. This was not an easy decision, but unfortunately, I do not have the time to maintain the add-on anymore. All the user data will be deleted on the same day.

I would recommend deleting the add-on before June 14 to avoid getting any errors related to the shutdown.

The code for the add-on, as well as the code for the API, will still be available as open-source on GitHub. If anyone wants to continue the project, they are of course invited to do so.

Thank you all for your support over the years, and thank you for your understanding.
